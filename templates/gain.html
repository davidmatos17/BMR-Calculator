Your Total Metabolic Rate (TMB) is the number of calories your body needs daily to maintain its current weight. To gain weight in a healthy manner, you need to consume more calories than your body burns while prioritizing nutrient quality and muscle growth.

Key Tips

🍽 Nutrition

  • Increase Calories: Add 300–500 kcal/day.
  • Nutrient-Dense Foods: Nuts, seeds, grains, lean proteins.
  • Frequent Meals: 5–6 smaller meals daily.

🏋️ Training

  • Strength Training: Focus on progressive overload.
  • Protein Intake: 1.6–2.2 g/kg body weight.
  • Muscle Growth: Resistance training + surplus calories.

📈 Consistency

  • Track Progress: Weigh yourself weekly.
  • Stay Consistent: Diet + training over time.
  • Adjust: Modify calories as needed.

Remember: gaining weight in a healthy way is gradual. Prioritize muscle over fat by combining proper nutrition with training and consistency.
